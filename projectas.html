ABOUT US

Our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 2020, BURGER POINT is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in kerala with about 18 restaurants operating in more than 10 districts.Our focus was on providing excellent food and utilising local suppliers. Alongside a choice of craft beers and great cocktails. You can’t be a great burger restaurant without great burgers. Every great burger restaurant has the burger that made them famous, the classics, as well as continuing to innovate new burger combinations. Great burger restaurants use high quality ingredients. Sauces and other ingredients have lots of flavor that can make up for less flavorful low quality meats, making it tempting to cut corners. However, using good meat and the right spices can enhance every burger even if it’s lathered in a million flavorful sauces. It also ensures that your plainest hamburgers taste great.
